Solution Design and implementation

From our experience, many clients are wary of renewable power systems because of their previous bad experience. At Actolog solutions we do not just rush to install power solar power systems, for an existing site, we would first of all deploy our state-of-the-arts Power Analyzer to the site and capture the power data of the clients. Our team of engineers will then analyze this data, discuss with the client and come up with a power solution that is tailored to the client’s needs and budget to ensure that we design and implement a power solution that will serve the clients need on the long term.

Our Power Solutions

The proposed solution provides Integrated Renewable Hybrid Power Solution (IRHPS) from 4 sources, viz;

  • High quality Solar power through Photovoltaics (PV) panels
  • Renewable energy storage (lithium-ion, VRLA, Gel) battery system
  • Public utility (grid) power supply
  • Power generators

This solution is designed and installed in such a way that the four sources of power are integrated and automated with solar power set as priority 1, then the batteries (priority 2), PHCN (priority 3) and then the diesel generator. In a fully automated and perfectly synchronized system, the solar panels will provide full power for the load during the day, and in low-lid (cloudy days) and during the night, stored energy in the batteries will supply power to the load through inverter, and in any case where the batteries drain below the set threshold, the public power (PHCN) source will kick in automatically, and in very rare cases, when the batteries are drained and there is neither sunlight nor PHCN, the system is designed to start up generator automatically to power the load and charge the battery bank for re-use.

With this system, it is estimated that about 40-50% of the power demand of a typical installation will come from solar power, 30% from stored battery power, 15% from PHCN and only 5% or less (if at all needed) would need to come from diesel generator. In this way, consumers can cut their energy cost by over 50% (as shown subsequently) while enjoying uninterruptible power supply 24/7.
